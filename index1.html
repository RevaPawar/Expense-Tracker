What is our Budget Tracker?

Budget tracking is the process of tracking expenses, receipts, and invoices so that a project can be completed within its allotted budget. Budget tracking can be done using budgeting and forecasting software. Project management software can also be used, in which case the budget details the anticipated expenses and profit for a specific project.
